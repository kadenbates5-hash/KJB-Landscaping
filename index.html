About KJB Landscaping

Established in 2022, KJB Landscaping was founded with a simple goal: to provide the Milwaukee community with high-quality property maintenance and landscaping solutions. We believe that a well-maintained outdoor space enhances the beauty and value of every property.

Our team is dedicated to precision, reliability, and professional excellence. Whether it is a weekly maintenance visit or a complete landscape redesign, we approach every project with the same level of care and attention to detail.
