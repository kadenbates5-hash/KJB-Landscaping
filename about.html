About KJB Landscaping

Quality Work, Professional Care, Rooted in Milwaukee.

Our Mission

At KJB Landscaping, our mission is simple: to provide high-quality landscaping services at fair prices while ensuring 100% customer satisfaction. We believe that every property deserves professional care, and we treat every yard as if it were our own.

We focus on precision, reliability, and excellence in everything we do, from basic lawn maintenance to complex hardscaping projects.

Our History

Established in 2022, KJB Landscaping began with a vision to bring fresh energy and dedicated service to the Milwaukee community. What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted local business known for its commitment to quality and attention to detail.

Since our founding, we have focused on building long-lasting relationships with our clients through honest work and exceptional results. We are proud to serve Milwaukee and the surrounding areas, helping our neighbors maintain beautiful and healthy outdoor spaces.
