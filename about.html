Established 2022

KJB Landscaping was officially established in 2022 to serve the Milwaukee area. What began as a local initiative has grown into a trusted name for property maintenance, built on the values of quality, reliability, and community service.

Our Quality Standards

We believe that landscaping is more than just maintaining a lawn—it is about preserving the aesthetic and functional value of your property. Our team uses professional-grade equipment and proven techniques to ensure that every job meets our rigorous quality standards.

We do not cut corners. From the crispness of our edging to the health of the garden beds we install, our focus is always on providing a superior service at a fair and honest price for our clients.
