Our Story

Built on ambition, hard work, and a commitment to Milwaukee.

Rooted in Excellence

Founded in 2022, KJB Landscaping was born out of a desire to provide the highest level of property maintenance to the Milwaukee area. What began as a local operation has quickly grown into one of the most successful and trusted landscaping names in the community.

We don't just mow lawns or clean gutters; we manage your property with the same precision and care that we would our own. Our growth is a testament to our quality over hustle approach.

Meet the Founder: Kaden Bates

KJB Landscaping isn't just a business—it's the result of a drive for excellence that starts at the top. Kaden Bates founded this company with a clear vision: to merge professional academic rigor with world-class manual service.
